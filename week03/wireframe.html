Canyon Run Rafting

At Canyon Run Rafting, we believe the river teaches us to be present — to feel the spray, hear the roar, and trust the journey. Our trips are designed not just for adrenaline, but for connection: to nature, to others, and to yourself. With expert guides and a deep respect for the wild, we make adventure safe, meaningful, and unforgettable.

Our History

Founded in 1995 by river guide Sam Reynolds, Canyon Run Rafting began as a dream to share the wild beauty of western rivers. What started with a single raft has grown into a trusted name in outdoor adventure, known for safety, sustainability, and soul-stirring experiences that stay with guests long after the journey ends.
